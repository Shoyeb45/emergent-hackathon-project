## Overview

Vivah AI is an all-in-one wedding management solution that brings together event planning, guest coordination, RSVP tracking, and intelligent photo management into a single seamless platform. Whether you're hosting or attending a wedding, Vivah AI makes the experience effortless and memorable.

## Key Features

### Wedding Organization
- Create and manage multiple wedding events
- Set up detailed event schedules with multiple ceremonies (Reception, Sangeet, Wedding Day, etc.)
- Specify venues, dates, and timings for each event
- Track all your hosted and invited weddings in one dashboard

### Smart Guest Management
- Send digital invitations via email
- Automated RSVP tracking system with status updates (Pending, Accepted, Declined)
- Manage guest lists with names and contact information
- Control photo upload permissions per guest
- Real-time RSVP status monitoring

### Event Scheduling
- Create comprehensive wedding timelines
- Add multiple events with specific dates, times, and locations
- Visual event calendar for easy planning
- Location mapping for each ceremony

### AI-Powered Photo Manager
The standout feature that sets Vivah AI apart:

- **Centralized Photo Gallery**: Host can upload all wedding photos to a shared gallery
- **Facial Recognition**: AI automatically classifies and organizes photos by detecting faces
- **Personalized Collections**: Guests can view all photos featuring them by simply uploading their photo
- **Smart Filtering**: AI fetches and displays all images where a specific guest appears
- **Two-Way Sharing**: 
  - Wedding Gallery: Host-uploaded professional photos
  - My Photos: Guest-uploaded personal moments
- **Upload Permissions**: Host controls which guests can contribute photos

## How It Works

### For Hosts

1. **Create a Wedding Event**
   - Click "Host a Wedding" and enter wedding details
   - Set up the venue and date.

2. **Build Your Schedule**
   - Add events like Reception, Sangeet, Wedding Day, etc.
   - Specify timing and location for each ceremony

3. **Invite Guests**
   - Add guests by name and email
   - System automatically sends invitation emails with RSVP links
   - Monitor responses in real-time

4. **Manage Photos**
   - Upload wedding photos to the central gallery
   - AI automatically organizes photos by faces detected
   - Control guest upload permissions

### For Guests

1. **Receive Invitation**
   - Get email invitation with event details and RSVP link

2. **Respond to RSVP**
   - Confirm or decline attendance with one click

3. **Access Your Photos**
   - Upload your photo to the platform
   - AI finds and displays all wedding photos featuring you
   - Download or share your personalized collection

4. **Upload Memories** (if permitted)
   - Share your own photos from the wedding
   - Contribute to the collective memory
